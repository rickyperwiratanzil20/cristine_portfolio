About me

SHE/HER

Cristine is a former Business Developer at a travel booking software company, having 2+ years of experience in the tech industry. In her last role, she found her true calling in UX through a couple of projects that she had delivered.

She undertook a UX Design course from UX Design Institute and obtained her Professional Diploma in UX Design certificate in 2021. However, her avidity for UX has outgrown and led her into the MSc Human Computer Interaction at UCD Dublin commencing in Sep 2022.

To seize the day, she receives regular mentorship from her senior mentors and learns a million things about UX in the real world because her goal is to mentor someone who pivots their career to UX one day, just like she did

Probably worth mentioning that she’s a passionate traveler, amateur photographer, and obsessed with tech and psychology.
